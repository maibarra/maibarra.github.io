About me

Only a few job descriptions can give you the amount of satisfaction Software Development's "Being in the Zone"

This is one of those areas that grows with you so organically. Like a virgin forest, like a desert cactus, but faster than the speed of light.

I started my work life like any graduate, looking for challenges up to what the job market requires, always with a solid logic base from my university's learning experience but, ready with openness to a philosophy of quick adaptation to an ever changing tech world.

After a few experiences that involved direct hardware & software support and development in a few experimental web technologies, I ended up at my city's public sector, where I became part of (and ultimately leading) the efforts of bringing government transactions with citizens up to international standards that improved the Citizen Experience (CX). Here, our work got several awards & acknowledgements that put our city in the top 3 places E-Government in our country.

At the same time, I gave back to my Alma Mater (University) teaching Software and Project management related subjects. I brought my students closer to a working view of Agile Methodologies, Software Engineering and Full-Stack Web development, among many subjects.

Now I'm looking to collaborate and build products with these 3 essential elements:

Usefulness, Growth & Happiness
